Legacy & Institution

MC WILLIAM
HIGH SCHOOL

MC WILLIAM HIGH SCHOOL was established in 1937 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Urban area. It is located in ALIPURDUAR MUNICIPALITY block of ALIPURDUAR district of West Bengal. The school consists of Grades from 5 to 12.

The school is Boys and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. Bengali is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.

The school has Government building. It has got 13 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall and electric connection.
